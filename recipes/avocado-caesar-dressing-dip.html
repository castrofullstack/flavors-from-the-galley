Avocado Caesar Dressing & Dip

A creamy, bright Caesar-style blend with avocado, Greek-style yogurt, anchovy, capers, lemon, Dijon, Pecorino Romano, and extra-virgin olive oil.

Ready in 20 minutes Serves 8 people Calories 280

Ingredients

What Goes In

  • 1 ripe avocado, pitted and peeled
  • 1/2 cup whole milk yogurt, Cabot Greek-style recommended
  • 1 full 2-oz can anchovy fillets, with the oil, 8-10 fillets
  • 1 tbsp capers, rinsed and drained
  • 2 small garlic cloves, peeled
  • 3 tbsp fresh lemon juice, about 1 lemon
  • 1 tbsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup finely grated Pecorino Romano cheese, plus extra for topping
  • As needed buttermilk, to thin for dip or dressing texture
  • To taste freshly ground black pepper

Preparation

Blend, Thin, Chill

  1. Blend the base: In a blender or food processor, combine the avocado, yogurt, anchovies with their oil, capers, garlic, lemon juice, mustard, Worcestershire, and Pecorino Romano. Blend until smooth.
  2. Emulsify: With the blender running, drizzle in the extra-virgin olive oil until fully incorporated and glossy.
  3. Adjust the texture: Add buttermilk gradually until you reach your desired texture: thick and creamy for dip, or looser and pourable for dressing.
  4. Taste and balance: Add more lemon for brightness, or a little more yogurt or avocado to mellow the flavor.
  5. Chill: Cover and chill at least 30 minutes before serving so the flavors settle.

Galley notes

Creamy, Bright, Deep

  • Anchovy oil and fillets bring bold umami depth.
  • Avocado and yogurt keep the texture creamy, balanced, and nutrient-dense.
  • Extra-virgin olive oil and Pecorino Romano bring classic Caesar richness and tang.
  • Lemon keeps the dressing bright, fresh, and ready for greens, vegetables, sandwiches, or grilled proteins.
